What is ALTERLY?

ALTERLY is a virtual entertainment agency launched by its parent agency WHIM Management, best known for managing its eSports team named EVOS Esports, in 2021. We are focused on producing, developing and nurturing talented virtual personalities to unlock a new generation and culture of entertainment and business line, transcending the physical boundaries and limitations and expand the audience through personalized virtual character.

Our mission is to introduce and build a new entertainment experience through virtual YouTuber (VTuber) & virtual characters.
